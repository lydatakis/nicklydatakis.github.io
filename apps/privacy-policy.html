Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2025

Last Updated: October 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to CodeMentor Quiz (the “App”). This Privacy Policy describes how the App handles information and explains the limited data that may be collected automatically by third-party services integrated within the App.

2. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or share personally identifiable information. However, certain non-personal data may be collected automatically by third-party services:

a. Google AdMob

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect information such as:

  • Advertising identifiers (e.g., Google Advertising ID)
  • Approximate location, IP address, and device information
  • Interaction data with ads

This data is processed according to Google’s Privacy Policy.

b. Google Play Services

Google Play Services may automatically collect anonymous diagnostics and analytics (such as crash reports) to improve performance. Learn more in Google’s Privacy Policy.

3. How Information Is Used

Any information collected through these services is used solely for:

  • Displaying personalized or non-personalized ads
  • App performance monitoring
  • Improving the user experience

The App developer does not sell, trade, or otherwise share data with external parties.

4. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for general audiences but is not directed toward children under 13 years of age. If you are under 13, please use the App under the supervision of a parent or guardian.

5. Data Security

The App does not directly store user data. Reasonable measures are taken to ensure that any information handled by integrated third-party services remains protected. Because these services are provided by independent companies, absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

6. Your Choices

You can manage or opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings:

  • Reset or delete your Advertising ID
  • Disable “Ad Personalization” in Google Settings

More information is available at https://adssettings.google.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with a new effective date. Continued use of the App after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
